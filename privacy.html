Privacy Policy

InvoiceMaker is built to be private by default. The invoices you create are generated entirely in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers. This page explains exactly what data is and isn't handled.

Your invoice data stays on your device

Everything you type into the invoice generator — your business details, client details, line items, totals, notes, and any logo you upload — is processed locally in your web browser. This information is never transmitted to us or stored on a server. When you download a PDF, the file is built on your own device using the open-source jsPDF library and saved directly to your computer. Refreshing or closing the page clears the data.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous usage — for example which pages are visited and roughly how many people use the tool. This helps us improve the site. Analytics does not capture the contents of your invoices. You can block analytics with most ad/tracker-blocking browser extensions.

Cookies and Advertising

This site uses cookies to improve your experience. We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
